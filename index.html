Long time no see, Winter Wonderland

We have not seen such a beautiful out-fashioned winter weather in Finland in

years and yet it happened almost in one night.

Aurora borealis. You can see aurora borealis in northern hemisphere. Auroras are seen the most clearly and often in high-latitude areas.

More snow is expected throughout a country

Many parts of the country may see at least from 10 to 50 centimeters of snow

depending the location. Road conditions are bad because of the snowstorm

and strong winds dropping the temperature to -10 degree Celsius or colder.

What is to expect:

Winter in 2020-2021 came late but it is expected to be long and harsh. Blackouts and delays in public transport are possible. Cold and snowy winter weather is seen worldwide. Temperature in Russia has been especially cold.
